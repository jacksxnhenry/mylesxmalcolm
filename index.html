Twin brothers Myles and Malcolm, raised outside San Francisco, shifted from college football dreams to music after life-changing injuries. Introduced to production by a cousin in high school, they immersed themselves in mixing, mastering, and songwriting. Though attending different colleges in Southern California, they stayed connected through music. Their debut EP, "Differences," was written, recorded, and released from two separate dorm rooms — a testament to their resilience and creative bond. Through their sound, they turn pain into purpose, transforming setbacks into sonic stories that reflect honesty, growth, and the journey of healing through art.
